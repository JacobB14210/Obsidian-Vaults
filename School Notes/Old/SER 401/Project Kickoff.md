# Things to do Before First Sponsor Meeting
- Read proposal
- Watch sponsor's video
- Create list of questions
- Research sponsor and sponsor company
- Prepare a preliminary agenda
- Determine who in your group will contact the sponsor
# Recommended Steps to Contacting Sponsor
- Request kickoff meeting
	- Propose a few times that might work
- Once a meeting is set, send a preliminary meeting agenda topics to cover
	- Questions you want answered
	- Ask for any items that the sponsor would like to add to the agenda
- Send calendar request with meeting date/time and the agenda
# Meeting Minutes Required Items
- Attendee List
- Date and time
- Topics covered
	- Requirements
	- Agreements reached
	- Required actions
	- Anything else talked about
- Action item list
- Date/Time of next meeting
# Meeting Minutes Assignment
- Meet with sponsor every 2 weeks
- Submit meeting minutes on canvas
- Best 5 sponsor meeting minutes will count towards team portion of your grade
# Project Plan Template
- Project description
	- Overview of client application
	- Statement of the problem
	- Context
- Design and architecture
- Implementation strategy
- Process
- Risk management
