# Key Executive for Product Development
- Chief Technology Officer (How can we make the impossible possible?)
	- Establishes company's technical vision and strategic direction
	- Conducts research on leading edge technologies
- Vice President of Engineering (Time is money)
	- Manages product design execution
	- Manages engineering budget
- Vice President of Marketing (What is the next big thing)
	- Conducts market research and competitor analysis
	- Develops and manages product pricing structures
