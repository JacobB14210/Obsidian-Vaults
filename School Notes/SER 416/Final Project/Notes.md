# Purpose
- Collect information about households in neighborhoods related to natural disasters
	- Identify at risk individuals or properties with critical safety risks
	- Send resources to prioritized at risk individual or property
	- Increase chance of survival
# Objectives and Success Criteria
- Stores, retrieves, and edits data about households within a neighborhood
- Local solution to avoid trying to use the internet during disasters
- Store data in the SQLite database to make it easy to implement in web based solution
- Provide cost effective solution
